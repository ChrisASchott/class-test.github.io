What's this all about anyway?

This site is a humble but growing collection of book reviews. The books considered here are collections of comics and books about comics. Comics include traditional newspaper strips and comic books as well as some that originated online.

Are there books you'd like to see reviewed? Do you have some relevant reviews of your own? General feedback? Major or private feedback? Look at our Contact page linked in the navigation bar at the top of the page.

Thanks for reading. See you in the funny pages.
